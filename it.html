Upstream — Enablers

Semiconductor equipment, EDA tools, and materials that make chip fabrication possible. Capex-cycle sensitive but structurally essential. Highest moats accrue to monopoly tool providers like ASML (EUV lithography) and the Synopsys/Cadence EDA duopoly.

Midstream — Silicon & Systems

Semiconductors, hardware platforms, networking, and storage. Physical product layer with cyclicality but increasing differentiation through custom silicon, AI accelerators, and advanced packaging. NVIDIA defines the AI capex cycle.

Downstream — Software & Services

Platform software, applications, cybersecurity, data infrastructure, and IT services. Highest margins, most recurring revenue, strongest network effects. Value accretes to platforms that become systems of record.

Enterprise Capex Cycle

Corporate IT spending budgets, server refresh cycles, and datacenter buildout pace. CIO surveys (Gartner, Morgan Stanley) provide leading indicators. Cloud migration and AI infrastructure are the primary capex vectors.

AI / ML Investment Wave

Hyperscaler AI capex (MSFT, GOOG, AMZN, META) is the most powerful demand signal cascading through all three tiers. GPU supply, custom silicon, and AI software monetization define the investment landscape.

Interest Rate Cycle

Fed funds rate and 10-year Treasury yield directly impact growth stock valuations (duration effect), enterprise IT budgets (borrowing cost), and M&A activity (financing availability). Rate cuts expand SaaS multiples.
